Site Plan - Travel Blog

Site Name

Travel Diaries

This name represents a blog that focuses on travel experiences, guides, and tips. It is catchy and easy to remember, making it ideal for branding and recognition.

Site Purpose

The site provides a platform for sharing travel experiences, offering destination guides, travel tips, and a photo gallery. It also allows visitors to submit their travel stories and engage with the content.

Scenariors

  • What are the top must-see destinations in Europe?
  • How can I submit my travel story to the blog?

Color Schema

Primary Color: #2E8B57 (used for headings and accents)

Secondary Color: #FAF0E6 (used for background and paragraphs)

Typography

Headings: Roboto, sans-serif

Body Text: Open Sans, sans-serif
